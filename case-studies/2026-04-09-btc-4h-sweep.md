BTC/USDT: 4hr Liquidity Sweep Analysis

Framework: Weekly sweep: 4h sweep: 15 min inducement/fvg

Strategy Logic:
Boxes are drawn on the wicks of the weekly liquidity sweep (CME must also properly wick)
4h candle wick (breaching weekly box) sweeps a 4h high (also breaching weekly box)
Box is drawn around that 4hr wick that sweeps
QTLResearch utilizes ICT concepts PD Array logic : 4h ---> 15 min
Identify first 15 min BOS inside 4h box
Identify IND (inducement): the first 15 min high made after the BOS
Draw equilibrium from 4h wick sweep high to recent low
Price must breach the equilibrium level
15 min must produce a FVG that breaches the IND level
The 15 min FVG breach of IND must also breach below the QTL level
